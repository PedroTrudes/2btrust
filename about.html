There has to be a better way to invest in real estate

Our goal is to make real estate investing as simple as investing in stocks or crypto. Diversification is key to any investment strategy, but the barrier to entry for real estate investing has always been so high. We don't believe that should be the case.

Jerry Chu

Founder & CEO

When I tried to buy my first rental property, I spent over two months flying to different cities, walking around the neighborhoods, setting up inspections, vetting property management companies, and more. The process was archaic and sucked up all of my time. When I talked to my friends, they seemed to have the same problem. The fact is, most people are prevented from investing in real estate due to the time commitment and high initial down-payment needed alongside the many operational requirements of managing a property.

That's why we're building a new way to invest in real estate that makes it 10x easier for people to start diversifying today. With Lofty you can buy tokens in cash flowing rental properties starting for just $50. All properties have a property manager in place and investors start earning rental income on day one. Life happens, so if you need to sell your tokens for any reason, there are no lock-up periods or minimums to sell.

Lofty is backed by a world class group of investors.

Our team brings decades of experience in real estate, data, technology, operations, and finance.
